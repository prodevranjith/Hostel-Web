Hostel Three Zero offers a convenient and affordable accommodation solution for students, travelers, and young professionals. Our Services

We always try to provide the best services available to our paying guests

We have an in-charge who takes care of the guests and ensures all facilities are in working condition.

Our facilities include battery inverters (providing reliable backup power during outages), a terrace dining area, 3 washing machines, and a terrace drying area.

Rooms

We have single, double, and triple sharing rooms with and without attached bathrooms, large windows for proper ventilation.

Security

The entire PG is monitored by CCTV, and the main entry is secured by a fingerprint sensor. Accessible 24/7 with fingerprint access.

Cleanliness

Rooms are cleaned 4 times a week, bathrooms are cleaned every day, and the whole PG is well-maintained.

Food

Food is made by the PG cook who prepares both North and South Indian dishes.
